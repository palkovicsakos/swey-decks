The two skills

Diagnose and decide, on command

/hormozi-advisor
Your personal strategist
Runs the 6 Ms, finds the ONE constraint, opens the matching KB doc, and hands back a single decision — every claim cited. A decision, not a menu.
When: "advise me on…", "what would Hormozi do", any of your own offer / leads / pricing / scaling questions.
e.g. "Advise me on the Targetive offer" → constraint named, framework applied, one move for this week.
/hormozi-review
Red-team a plan
Restates the plan in ≤5 bullets, runs the 6 Ms + Lead-Cost flowchart, names the one binding constraint, then attacks only that link — ranked fixes plus the bet, stated out loud.
When: "stress-test this", "red-team my funnel", "what's my constraint", "where does this cap out".
e.g. "Red-team my Q3 plan" → "Constraint = Sales, not leads. Here's where it caps, and the 3 fixes."
